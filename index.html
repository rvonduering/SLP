Rebecca von Duering, MS, CCC-SLP

A life-long learner and educator for 20+ years, I'm embarking on my next journey- Private Practice SLP with a specialty in Gender Affirming Voice. My career began in Elementary Education but I quickly found my passion in Speech-Langauge Pathology starting in 2008. In 2015, I became a CLinical Lead and I developed the Gender Affirming Voice program for Kaiser Permanente of Washington. Since then, I've helped hundreds of people affirm their gender using safe, competent, and evidence-based treatment approaches to address pitch, resonance, acoustics, inflection, prosody, and co-occuring voice disorders through individualized care plans.

Speech and Language Pathology

A highly trained inpatient and outpatient SLP since 2008, I have special certifications in the PESL Accent Modification, SOS Approach to Feeding, MBSImp protocol for Modified Barium Swallow Studies, LSVT LOUD, PhoRTE, Hanen: It Takes Two to Talk and More Than Words, and Gender Affirming Voice Therapy.
